# Carousel

A simple carousel component. Although auto play and looping are supported, they should be used with caution as these features can be distracting and annoying to users.

### Basic usage

```html
<et-carousel>
  <et-carousel-item>
    <img src="https://source.unsplash.com/random/1220x600" alt="img" />
  </et-carousel-item>
  <et-carousel-item autoPlayTime="7500">
    <img src="https://source.unsplash.com/random/1230x600" alt="img" />
    <button>Focus me</button>
  </et-carousel-item>
  <et-carousel-item>
    <img src="https://source.unsplash.com/random/1260x600" alt="img" />
  </et-carousel-item>

  <div>
    <button etCarouselPreviousButton>Prev</button>
    <button etCarouselNextButton>Next</button>
    <button etCarouselToggleAutoPlayButton>Play/Pause</button>
    <et-carousel-item-nav />
  </div>
</et-carousel>
```

### Inputs

---

#### loop (boolean)

Loop play of carousel items

---

#### autoPlay (boolean)

Automatic play of carousel items

---

#### autoPlayTime (number)

Play time per carousel item \
**_note:_** \
default: 5000ms

---

#### pauseAutoPlayOnHover (boolean)

Pause auto play of carousel items on hover

---

#### pauseAutoPlayOnFocus (boolean)

Pause auto play of carousel items on focus

---

#### transitionDuration (number)

Transition time between carousel items \
**_note:_** \
default: 450ms
